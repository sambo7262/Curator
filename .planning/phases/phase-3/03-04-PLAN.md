<objective>
Build the two pure quality gates that run before any download: the profile/cutoff format-bitrate gate (no downgrades) and the coarse fake/transcoded-FLAC sanity check. Both consume the neutral Candidate + Profile types and emit reasons; both skip (never reject) on absent data.

Purpose: QUAL-01 (the neutral Profile/QualityRank the adapter normalizes into and core consumes — the firewall-clean side), QUAL-02 (filter by format/bitrate before download, never below cutoff), QUAL-03 (coarse fake-FLAC heuristics, not spectral). These are structural pre-download defenses.
Output: app/core/quality.py (Profile + QualityRank + gate), app/core/fakeflac.py (check), and their corpus-driven tests.
</objective>
