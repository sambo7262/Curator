<objective>
Build the phase's hard core: the deterministic, pure, explainable matcher that ports the beets weighted-distance model and the rec-gap recommendation, scoring noisy slskd candidates against the authoritative manifest and producing accept/decline with reasons. Purpose: This is MATCH-01 (score on authoritative identity incl. track-count completeness, select best) and MATCH-02 (decline below a conservative configurable threshold — precision over recall, the headline guarantee). It is a pure function with no I/O, no network, no clock.
Output: app/core/matching.py (score + recommend) and test_matching.py proving the labeled corpus decisions.
</objective>
